🌻 Nayyab · To My Everlasting Sunflower 🌻
Two blooms that leaned across the sunlit air,
Your golden gaze, my silent prayer.
Like petals turned to find the light,
I found my home in you each night.

For every storm that passed us by,
I'll grow again beneath your sky.
Forgive me, love — and stay right here,
Your sunflower heart, forever near.
✨ Forever rooting for you — Your Sun ✨
